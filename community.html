Our commitment extends beyond production to the wellbeing of the local communities we serve. We focus on:

  • Affordable Dairy Access: Ensuring nutritious milk and dairy products are available at prices that benefit families and schools.
  • Nutrition Education: Running workshops and campaigns to promote healthy dietary habits, especially for children and vulnerable groups.
  • Job Creation: Generating employment opportunities across the dairy value chain, from farming to processing and retail.
  • Community Empowerment: Supporting local initiatives and cooperatives that drive economic growth and social cohesion.

Through these efforts, AERO DAIRY strengthens the fabric of our communities, improving health, livelihoods, and social sustainability.
