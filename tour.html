Welcome to the AERO DAIRY Platform Tour

Platform Overview

AERO DAIRY provides a unified, interactive dashboard tailored to streamline dairy education and operational excellence. Whether you're a student, admin, or partner, this platform gives you tools to manage programs, admissions, permits, and more—all from one central hub.

Program Access

Explore a range of structured modules covering Dairy Farming, Animal Health, Milk Processing, and more. Each program is designed with visual learning, practical steps, and real-world applications to help farmers, students, and entrepreneurs thrive.

Admissions Process

Admission is seamless and fully online. Users fill out a form, upload necessary documents, and receive confirmation—all within minutes. The admin backend allows quick review and approval or follow-up of applications.

Report Insights

Track dairy progress with visual reports and monthly insights. Metrics include user engagement, production trends, training performance, and funding status—all accessible through dynamic dashboards and downloadable formats.

Contact & Support

If you ever need assistance, the platform provides a direct messaging feature, social channels, and FAQs. Support is available through WhatsApp, Email, and scheduled calls. Our goal is to ensure you're always connected to the right resources.
