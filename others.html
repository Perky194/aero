Community Empowerment Programs

AERO DAIRY supports grassroots dairy cooperatives and women's groups with direct training, equipment leasing, and technical advisory services. These initiatives empower smallholders to scale their operations sustainably.

  • Field-based dairy extension services
  • Women-led milk collection centers
  • Public–private partnerships with county agricultural departments

Research & Innovation Outreach

We actively collaborate with universities and research institutes to test, implement, and publish modern practices that improve animal welfare, milk yields, and farm sustainability.

Recent focus areas include:

  • Zero-grazing vs open pasture effectiveness trials
  • Probiotic feed additives for disease resistance
  • Blockchain-backed milk traceability systems

Educational Partnerships

Through joint programs with vocational colleges, we offer certified courses in dairy technology, veterinary assistance, and farm management.

Graduates receive certification and internship placement in farms associated with AERO DAIRY.

Environmental Sustainability Drives

We champion eco-friendly practices through solar-powered coolers, biogas systems from cow dung, and water recycling units on pilot farms.

  • Solar milk chillers reduce diesel costs by 60%
  • Waste-to-energy conversion supports green processing
  • Eco-certification for farms adopting 80% green compliance
