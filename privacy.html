AERO DAIRY Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when interacting with AERO DAIRY platforms—such as your name, email, phone number, area of study, and admission-related information. We may also collect usage data such as pages visited and device type.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for managing your application, communication, improving service quality, and responding to inquiries. We may also use aggregated data for research or reporting, without identifying individuals.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your user experience and to track website usage through analytics tools. You may disable cookies via your browser, although some features may become limited.

4. Data Protection & Sharing

We protect your data through secure systems and encryption. Your information will never be sold or shared without your permission, unless required by law or for critical service delivery purposes (e.g., payment processors).

5. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To do so, contact us at info@aerodairy.com. You may also opt out of communications at any time.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in legal or operational requirements. Continued use of our services implies acceptance of the updated policy.
