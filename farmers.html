At AERO DAIRY, we empower farmers with practical and innovative solutions to enhance dairy production. Our comprehensive support includes:

  • Animal Health Management: We offer training and resources on disease prevention, vaccination schedules, and veterinary care to keep herds healthy and productive.
  • Feed Efficiency: Guidance on optimal feeding techniques and nutrition plans to maximize milk yield and animal welfare.
  • Milk Preservation: Introduction to cooling technologies and hygienic storage methods to maintain milk quality from farm to market.
  • Record Keeping Systems: Digital tools and workshops to help farmers track herd performance, milk sales, and expenses efficiently.
  • Financial Literacy: Assistance with budgeting, accessing credit, and improving profitability through better farm management.

Our farmer-focused initiatives not only increase milk production but also boost livelihoods, ensuring sustainability and resilience in the dairy sector.
