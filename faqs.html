FAQs

Your common queries answered in one place.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is AERO DAIRY?
AERO DAIRY is a sustainable dairy initiative providing quality milk and comprehensive training for farmers and students.
2. Where is AERO DAIRY located?
We are headquartered in Nyandarua County, Kenya, with outreach programs country‑wide.
3. How do I apply for a program?
Visit the Admission page on our dashboard, click Apply Now, complete the form, and submit.
4. Are scholarships available?
Yes. Scholarships are offered based on merit and need; check announcements in News & Events.
5. What programs are offered?
Programs include Dairy Farming, Milk Processing, Animal Health, Agribusiness Management, and more.
6. Can I visit the farm?
Absolutely. Guided tours run monthly. Book a slot in the Tour section.
7. How do I contact support?
Email us at info@aerodairy.com or call +254 700 123 456.
8. Is AERO DAIRY certified?
Yes, we comply with Kenyan dairy regulations and international quality standards.
9. How is data privacy handled?
Your data is protected under our strict Privacy Policy.
10. Can I partner with AERO DAIRY?
Yes! Reach out via the Leadership contact on our About Us page.
11. What payment methods are accepted?
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, major credit/debit cards, and selected mobile wallets.
12. Do you offer internships?
Internships run quarterly. Check the Programs page for open slots.
13. Can I order milk online?
Home delivery is in pilot phase. Subscribe to our newsletter for launch updates.
14. How frequently is the News section updated?
Weekly, covering events, innovations, and success stories.
15. What is your refund policy?
Refunds are processed case‑by‑case within 14 working days after a formal request.
16. Can I volunteer?
Yes! Submit your interest via the Contact Us page and we’ll get back to you.
17. Do you offer online learning?
An e‑learning portal is under development and will launch soon.
18. Which languages do you support?
We currently support English and Kiswahili, with plans for local dialects.
19. Can I get farm investment advice?
Yes, our agribusiness team provides expert consulting through the Farmer Portal.
20. How do I report an issue?
Use the Contact Us form or email us directly with detailed information.
