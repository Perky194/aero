Sustainability Practices in Dairy

Champion carbon‑neutral, regenerative, and climate‑smart dairy systems for a resilient future.

Program Details

Overview

This program empowers dairy professionals to embed environmental stewardship across every stage of the production chain. Learners explore regenerative grazing, energy‑efficient processing, waste‑water valorization, and carbon‑neutral supply‑chain design.

Learning Outcomes

  • Quantify and reduce farm‑to‑table greenhouse‑gas (GHG) emissions.
  • Implement regenerative grazing plans to restore soil carbon and biodiversity.
  • Convert manure and whey into value‑added biogas, fertilizer, or bio‑based materials.
  • Secure sustainability certifications (e.g., ISO 14001, PAS 2060, Rainforest Alliance).

Curriculum Highlights

  • Life‑Cycle Assessment & Carbon Foot‑Printing
  • Renewable Energy for Dairy (Solar, Biogas, Geo‑thermal)
  • Water Stewardship & Circular Processing Technologies
  • Regenerative Pasture & Agro‑forestry Integration
  • Sustainable Packaging and Supply‑Chain Traceability

Practical Activities

Participants conduct LCA audits on partner farms, design solar‑powered chilling units, and pilot bio‑digesters that turn waste into power and fertilizer. Field visits showcase carbon‑insetting projects with cooperative producers.

Career Opportunities

Graduates become sustainability managers, carbon analysts, regenerative ranch consultants, or ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) officers in dairy companies, NGOs, and governmental agencies.
