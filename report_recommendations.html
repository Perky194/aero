Final Recommendations Report

Based on the data collected from traffic, user behavior, conversions, and questions, the following are strategic recommendations for improving AERO DAIRY's digital engagement:

  • 1. Boost Engagement on Top Programs:
    Use video content, testimonials, and real-time Q&A to increase time on page for programs like Dairy Farming and Milk Processing.
  • 2. Improve Form Conversion Rates:
    Simplify forms, add progress indicators, and reduce required fields to make it easier for users to submit inquiries or apply.
  • 3. Target FAQs More Directly:
    Add a dynamic FAQ section on the homepage or program pages based on the most common user questions to reduce bounce rate.
  • 4. Leverage Email Campaigns:
    With strong email subscription conversions, initiate monthly newsletters with updates, tips, and training announcements.
  • 5. Promote Resource Downloads:
    Use call-to-action banners across the site to increase downloads of PDF guides and learning materials.
  • 6. Expand Mobile Optimization:
    Since users increasingly access the site via mobile, ensure all pages load fast and are touch-friendly.
  • 7. Set Up Google Analytics Goals:
    Track program applications, contact forms, and downloads as conversion goals to evaluate marketing impact more precisely.
  • 8. Introduce Live Chat or WhatsApp Widget:
    Enable users to instantly ask questions, especially on high-traffic pages like Animal Health and Program Admission.
