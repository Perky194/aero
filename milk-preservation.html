Introduction

Ensuring milk stays fresh and safe requires precise methods and equipment:

Key Techniques

  • Refrigeration to 4°C immediately post-milking
  • Pasteurization at 72°C for 15s
  • Deep chilling (1–2°C) for extended shelf life
  • UHT treatment (135°C for 2s) for long‑life milk
  • Vacuum packaging to reduce oxidation
  • Cold‑chain transport with temperature logging

Storage Methods

Quality Checks

  • pH: 6.6–6.8 daily
  • Acidity: 0.13–0.17% each batch
  • Somatic cells: <200,000/ml weekly
  • Bacteria <100,000/ml weekly

Advanced & Future

  • Solar chillers for off‐grid farms
  • GPS‐tracked cold vehicles
  • Blockchain traceability
  • Staff certification & audits
